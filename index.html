About Me

Hi! My name is Cody Schleif. With a background in technical sales, I found my passion in programming. As a sales person I've had to use a lot of clunky and counter-intuitive software. Curious as to why these programs worked the way they do, I started teaching myself how to code. Almost immediately, I knew this is what I wanted to do with my life! I love the combination of creative and logical problem-solving skills I get to use when writing code. Since I started coding, I've taken as many online courses as I can manage, in the process of completing Udacity's Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree, and built some awesome projects!
